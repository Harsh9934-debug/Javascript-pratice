Benefits of All Vacuum-Packed Pulses

Posted on February 7, 2026 | By PureAmrut Team

When you buy pulses (dals) from the market, you often find small insects or dust inside the packet after a few weeks. This happens because traditional packing exposes grains to air and moisture. At PureAmrut, we have revolutionized this with our Vacuum Packing Technology.

1. Zero Insect Infestation

Insects and pests need oxygen to survive. Our vacuum packing process removes all air from the packet, creating an environment where weevils, beetles, and other common pests simply cannot live. This means your dals stay 100% bug-free for months without any artificial preservatives.

2. Locks in Freshness & Nutrition

Air (oxidation) is the enemy of freshness. It degrades the natural oils and nutrients in pulses. By removing air, we lock in the fresh, farm-picked taste and nutritional value of the pulses. When you open a PureAmrut pack, it smells as fresh as the day it was harvested.

3. Extended Shelf Life

Traditional loose pulses absorb moisture from the air, leading to spoilage and fungus. Vacuum packing acts as a barrier against humidity, significantly extending the shelf life of the product. Whether it's monsoon or summer, your pulses remain dry and safe.
